This is Apple

Apples are one of the most popular and widely consumed fruits in the world. They come in a variety of colors, including red, green, and yellow, and have a crisp texture with a sweet or slightly tart taste. Apples are rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, making them a healthy choice for a snack. They are commonly eaten fresh, made into juices, or used in desserts like apple pie and applesauce. The saying "an apple a day keeps the doctor away" highlights their nutritional benefits.
