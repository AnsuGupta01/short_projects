This is Banana

Bananas are a tropical fruit known for their soft texture, natural sweetness, and high energy content. They are an excellent source of potassium, which helps maintain heart health and proper muscle function. Bananas are easy to digest and are often recommended as a quick snack or part of a healthy breakfast.They can be eaten raw, blended into smoothies, or used in baking, such as banana bread. Their convenient natural packaging makes them one of the most portable and hassle-free fruits.
