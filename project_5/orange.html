This is orange

Oranges are citrus fruits known for their juicy, tangy-sweet flavor and high vitamin C content. They are essential for boosting the immune system and promoting healthy skin. Oranges can be enjoyed in various forms, including fresh slices, juice, and zest, which is often used for flavoring desserts and dishes. They are widely grown in warm climates and are popular as a refreshing snack. The bright orange color and refreshing taste make them a favorite fruit for many people worldwide.
